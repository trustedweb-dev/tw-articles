This page shows the "Sharing user trust across platforms in mutual aid services" exploried and tested by Dai Nippon Printing Co., Ltd., in the 2022 "Use Case Demonstration Project of the Trusted Web".

Please click on the links at the bottom of this page to learn more about the details of the demonstration.

Current issues and What Trusted Web Solves



Data to verify

  • Issue the achievement of activities in the mutual help app as a digital certificate. Verify the authenticity of the achievement by VC signature to enhance the supporter’s trust. In particular, VC issued by mutual help app A will be verified by other mutual help app B.
  • We assume submission of digital certificates for mutual help activity achievement to universities and companies to create new value in future.

Achievements

  • With demonstration on system planning and development, we realized the needs to “clarify security concerns” and required “private key recovery method of users”. With clarifying security concerns, we studied concerns on both VC and DID. We sorted out 4 directions based on result of ID-ideal research of Germany, namely, “recovery by oneself” , ”intensive recovery”, ”recovery by the trusted third”, “not recoverable (no recovery)”. We plan to adopt an ”intensive recovery” method by using Cloud Wallet
  • With demonstration on business model, there are 4 issues on “safety”, “awareness”, “continued use (incentive) “and “monetize”. By building echo system of mutual help app, we can contribute “safety” and “continued use (incentive) “.
  • With demonstration on the formulation of Mutual help trust framework, we studied “A. clarify items of mutual help trust framework”, “B. meaning of the formulation of mutual help trust framework”, “C. clarify the role of each stakeholder”, “D. Issues on the formulation of Mutual help trust framework”. We will continue study on the item D as there are still issues on trust certification criteria and UI/UX of app used by actual users.
