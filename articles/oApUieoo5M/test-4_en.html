This use case is a corporate tax system and an industrial association certificate. In the corporate tax system, in addition to the tax content stipulated by the Corporate Tax Law, there is a policy tax system under the Special Taxation Measures Law, in which measures such as tax reduction measures are taken for the purpose of economic revitalization. In this use case, among this policy tax system, the small and medium-sized enterprise management strengthening tax system [Type A] (hereinafter referred to as this tax system) linked to the Small and Medium Enterprise Management Enhancement Act (referred to as this project).
